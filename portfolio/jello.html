JELL-O

For my Junior year I chose to concentrate on Branding. One of the projects we have had is to choose a current brand that is failing and rebrand it. I chose the family favorite JELL-O.

I looked into a lot of JELL-Oâ€™s current branding and spent time seeing where they were failing and how I could change that. I found the company was spending too much time marketing to children. As sugary treats from the past I felt that they had a better market with people between the ages of 18-35. I decided to re-brand their packaging with a more sophiticated feeling to it.

I decided to re package three of the most popular JELL-O flavours. I also created a fictional line of JELL-O Mystery to add a new item to the brand that would catch attention. The third item I chose to focus on was the already marketed JELL-O Play. It is a mold set for building with and while I loved the idea I felt that it needed a cleaner look, much like the other boxes.
